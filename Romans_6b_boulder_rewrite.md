**Romans 6b – Freed From Sin, Slaves to Righteousness**

This is how most people talk about their sin—like it’s a little loose gravel on the road. But God sees it for what it is—a massive boulder blocking the way. A boulder you can’t move, can’t get around, and can’t ignore. And unless it’s removed, it will keep you from the destination He’s called you to.
**INTRODUCTION**
Before we dive into Romans 6, I want us to remember why this matters for the nations. Just as that boulder blocks a road, sin blocks the way to God for every people group still in darkness...
**Point 1 – Grace Exposes Your Master**
Grace doesn’t hand you a broom for the gravel—it sends the demolition crew for the boulder. When Christ saves you, He removes not just the penalty of sin, but the master you once served.
**CONCLUSION**
So the question is this—are you still camped at the foot of the boulder, pretending it’s just a minor inconvenience? Or have you followed the King who shattered it and brought you under His banner?
