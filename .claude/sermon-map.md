# Sermon Reference Map

A complete catalog of every sermon, teaching, and study in the repository — organized by Bible book and passage. Includes both the Romans series (primary manuscripts in the root folder) and the full archive (Other-Sermons/).

---

## How to Use This Map

- **By passage**: Find what's been preached on any Bible text
- **By subject**: Scan for topical coverage (evangelism, suffering, election, etc.)
- **Avoid repetition**: Check before reusing an illustration or cross-reference
- **Find source material**: Locate earlier treatments of a passage for reuse or revision
