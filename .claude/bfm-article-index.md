## Preaching Gaps — BFM Articles Not Yet Covered

The following articles represent clear preaching opportunities:

1. **Article VII — Baptism and the Lord's Supper**: Both ordinances are practiced and referenced frequently, but a dedicated BFM exposition would formalize the church's understanding.
2. **Article X — Last Things**: Extensive eschatological preaching exists but has never been tied to the BFM framework.
3. **Article XII — Education**: No coverage. Could pair with Proverbs or 2 Timothy 2:15.
4. **Article XIII — Stewardship**: Themes present but no BFM-framed sermon.
5. **Article XVI — Peace and War**: No coverage. Romans 13 treatment is adjacent.
6. **Article XVIII — The Family**: Adoption theology and godly parenting preached but not under BFM framework.
