## Cross-Chain Observations

### Chains That Are Complete
- **Wrath → Mercy** (Chain 8): The Romans series covers this end to end. No gaps.
- **Law → Grace** (Chain 4): Only missing a standalone Exodus 20 sermon; the doctrine is fully covered through Romans and Galatians.

### Chains With Critical Gaps
- **Passover → Cross** (Chain 3): 4 of 6 steps unpreached. Isaiah 53 and Exodus 12 are the most significant absences — these are foundational to the atonement theology that Romans 3:25 assumes.
- **Adam → Christ** (Chain 1): 1 Corinthians 15 (bodily resurrection) is the #2 preaching gap in the gap analysis.

### Chains Feeding Future Sermon Planning
- **Election → Glory** (Chain 5): Ephesians 1:3-14 is #6 in preaching gap analysis.
- **Exile → Home** (Chain 6): Revelation 21-22 is #3 in preaching gap analysis.
- **Shepherd → Lamb** (Chain 7): Isaiah 40 is #9 in preaching gap analysis.
