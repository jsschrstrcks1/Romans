# Theological Commitments

This series is anchored in:

## Soteriology (Salvation)
- Penal substitutionary atonement
- Justification by faith alone (sola fide)
- Grace alone (sola gratia)
- Monergistic regeneration (God initiates, we respond)

## Hamartiology (Sin)
- Total depravity: active rebellion, not passive ignorance
- No neutrality: always serving sin or righteousness
- Heart is deceitful above all things

## Theology Proper (God)
- Divine sovereignty over all things
- Election and predestination (Calvinistic, but pastoral)
- God's wrath as holy revulsion, not petty rage
- Foreknowledge as fore-love, not mere foresight

## Ecclesiology (Church)
- Body of Christ with diverse gifts
- Great Commission belongs to every believer
- Local church as gathered community under Word
