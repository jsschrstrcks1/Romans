Translation:

7. Loved ones not command new I write to you but command old which you had from beginning the command the old is the word which you heard. 
Again command new I write to you that is true in him and in you that the dark leads along and the light and the true already shines the ones saying in the light to be and the brother of him hating in the dark he is until now. The one loving the brother of him in the light stays and offense in him not there is. The but one hating the brother of him in the dark he is and in the dark walks around and not he knows where he goes off because the dark blinded the eyes of him. I write to you little children because have been sent off to you the sins through the name of him. I write to you fathers because you have known the one from beginning I write to you young men because you have conquered the evil. I wrote to you small children because you have known the father. I wrote to you fathers because you have known the one from the beginning. I wrote to you young men because strong you are and the word of the God in you stays and you have conquered the evil. 

Improved: 
7. Loved ones I am not writing a new command for you, but an old one which you have had from the beginning. The old commandment is the word which you have heard. 
8. Again I am writing to you a new command that is true in Him and in you, that the dark leads along, (and yet) the true light is already shining. 
9. The ones who say they are in the light and yet hate their brother are in the dark until now. 
10. The one who loves his brother abides in the Light and there is no offense (in having done so.).
11. But the one hating his brother is in the dark, he walks in the dark and he does not know where he goes because the darkness has blinded his eyes.
12. I am writing you little (dear?) children because your sins have been sent away because of His name. 
13. I write to you fathers because you have known the One who (has existed) from the beginning  I write to you young men because you conquered the evil (one?). 
14. I wrote to you fathers because you have known the One who has been from the beginning. I wrote to you young men because you are strong and the word of God abides in you, and you have conquered the evil (one?).
