## Pseudepigrapha and False Teaching

- Three groups of ancient books: Canon (66 books), Apocrypha (included by Catholics and Orthodox), Pseudepigrapha (claim to be scripture but verifiably false)
- Language dates documents — "groovy" (60s), "bad" meaning good (80s), "booyah/psych/fly" (90s), "bae/lit/snowflake" (2010s)
- If document claims Paul wrote it but uses modern language — fake
- **Examples from non-canonical literature**:
  - *Book of Tobit* (Apocrypha, not pseudepigrapha — Catholics consider it canonical): Sarah married 7 times, each husband killed by the demon Asmodeus on their wedding night; blind man + angel Raphael burns fish liver/heart to scare demon — roots of mumbling nonsense to bind demons
  - *Testament of Solomon* (pseudepigrapha, 1st-3rd century AD): Solomon's ring to bind demons, interrogating demons for their names and weakness words; ⚠️ demon names as preached ("Orniskelos," "Hellorath") appear garbled — the text has *Onoskelis* (a female demon) and possibly *Rath* (a lion-formed spirit); ring broken when Solomon dallied with female demons

## What Scripture Actually Teaches

- 1 John 4:4: "he who is in you is greater than he who is in the world" (ESV)
- Colossians 1:13-14: "He has delivered us from the domain of darkness and transferred us to the kingdom of his beloved Son, in whom we have redemption, the forgiveness of sins." (ESV)
- Ephesians 1:13-14: "you ... were sealed with the promised Holy Spirit, who is the guarantee of our inheritance" (ESV) — believers cannot be demon-possessed
- Angels are sentient tools in God's hands — hero is God, not angels
- **Cargo cult analogy**: Pacific Island peoples worshipped cargo deliveries instead of the God behind them — don't worship the boat

## Dualism Heresy

- Everything as galactic fight between angels and demons — fails by leaving out sovereign God
- Battle already won; we are sealed/protected by Holy Spirit
- Used to remove individual responsibility for sin
- "The devil made me do it" — origins in the Garden: "The woman said, 'The serpent deceived me, and I ate'" (Gen 3:13); "The man said, 'The woman whom you gave to be with me, she gave me fruit of the tree, and I ate'" (Gen 3:12)
- **Personal application**: Devil didn't break the lawn mower, trucks, Buick, AC, Hyundai brakes; didn't give you gas or make you sign up for irresponsible credit card; didn't make you embrace life of sin — "You chose that. You are responsible for that."

## Riches of God (from Eph 1)

- Lottery winners article: worst thing that happened to most — spending, shysters, debt, die alone
- God's riches: every euro, pound, yen, dollar belongs to God; your house = "God's house he lets you live in"
- Beyond material: perfect goodness, grace, patience — all treasures, all costly to learn
- "You only want to ask for patience if you're ready to encounter situations that make you impatient"
- God lavishes on us "in accordance with his wisdom"
