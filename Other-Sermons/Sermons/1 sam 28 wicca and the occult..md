In his desperation, he turns to a medium. 

A medium in Hebrew is Oboth. Oboth is a word with a lot of definitions. Depending on the little dots, and lines above and below it are placed it can mean a lot of things. Whore, it can mean or concubine. But in scripture its only used four ways, and the most common way that it is used is of one who seeks out evil spirits for their own ends. In this specific instance, Oboth is translated medium, because Saul is seeking out one who can talk to a dead person. 

There are basically two types of mediums out there today. Those that are fake. I think this is probably the bulk of them. And those that talk to demons. As a Christian, do we want to give our time, money and attention to either? 

This woman Saul found seems to be in a third category. Pay attention when we get to verse 12 though. I think this might be the first time her quote unquote powers actually worked. It startles her and she screams.
