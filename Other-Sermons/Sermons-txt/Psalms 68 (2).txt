I love this verse. For a number of reasons, one of those is that it reminds me of Plato. Have you ever heard of the philosopher plato? He is famous for a lot of things, but the one we are going to focus on is his idea of ideal states. Its like this. There are a lot of different kinds of chairs in the world right? But, plato would argue, there is one chair, and ideal chair. A perfect chair that exists out there that all of the other chairs are lesser derivations of. A heavenly chair. All the others, while good, and still chairs, are just frail copies of that one perfect, or ideal chair. 
‌
The same is true of God. Your’s oh Lord is THE greatness. The Power, THE glory, THE victory, and THE Majesty. All of the other greatnesses on earth, they pale in comparison to the greatness that is God’s. what the opposite of great? Small? Tiny? Minor? Insignificant? If God is great, the greatness of man is inconsequential compared to Gods. Our greatness is like the flame of a tiny candle, compared to the hurricane of God’s greatness. Ive been reading about that solar flare, the coronal mass ejection that happened not so long ago. By some systems of measurement it was the biggest one since the 1930s, by others the biggest since the 50s. Regardless that had me curious. How much material was ejected by that CME. Turns out it was 7 large coronoal mass ejections. So it goes ones, tens, hundreds, thousands, hundred thousands, millions, billions, trillions, and then quadrillions, two and a half quadrillions. Or thousands of trillions of pounds of super heated, material from the sun hit the earth all at once. But the sun is so big, it doesn’t even notice a few quadrillion pounds of weight coming off. It isn’t any dimmer. It doesn’t provide any less warmth. Us compared to God, and this is inadequate to fully describe the situation, its selling things far short. Us compared to God is like a dim little candle, next to the sun. God’s enemies are no better. Brittle, easily shattered. Like a pane of glass, to God’s hammer. What can be great compared to him?
