Hows that going to make her feel?
True love is not driven by obligation, -  I have to do this. True love is driven by passion – I want to do this.

Coming to church, going through the motions, out of obligation doesn’t honor God. We cant honor God by worship out of obligation. Its not satisfying to us, its not satisfying to God. We honor God by worship out of love and passion, When we behold the honor, and power and glory of God

IT seems natural that we should love our savior right off the bat, but if you really think about it, that’s not neccesarily the case right away. If you’re drowing and a captain orders a life ring be tossed to you so you don’t drown, you are surely greatful, you surely glad he was there, glad you didn’t drown. But you don’t automatically love the captain that ordered your saving. Over time you can come to love him. But its not instant. It takes time and effort to really love someone. Men when you’re courting the woman you love, you put in some time thinking about her, what she would like and wouldn’t like, time in doing things for her that she would like, when you love someone you make an effort.
